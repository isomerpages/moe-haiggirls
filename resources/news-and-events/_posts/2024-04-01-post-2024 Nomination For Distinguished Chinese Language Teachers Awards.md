<p>&nbsp;We would like to invite you to nominate our Chinese Language teachers
for the Distinguished Chinese Language Teachers Awards.</p>
<p>Nominate your Chinese Language teacher who:</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; inspire students to
have an interest in Chinese language and culture</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; use creative and engaging
teaching methods / learning activities</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; have a passion for teaching</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; respected and well-loved
by students, parents and fellow teachers</p>
<p>Nominated teachers should have at least 5 years of teaching experience
in MOE schools. This&nbsp;award&nbsp;serves to recognise deserving&nbsp;Chinese&nbsp;Language&nbsp;teachers&nbsp;who
have inspired students to have an interest in&nbsp;Chinese&nbsp;language
and culture, and have used creative and engaging&nbsp;teaching&nbsp;methods
and/or learning activities with their students.</p>
<p>You may nominate your&nbsp;Chinese&nbsp;Language teachers who are respected
and well-loved by students <a href="https://www.gevme.com/nomination-form-DCLTA2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here.</a>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;Registration will close on Sunday, 12 May 2024.</p>
