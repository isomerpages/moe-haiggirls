<h2><strong>Application for Parent Volunteer for P1 Admission in 2028</strong></h2>
<p>Are you interested in joining our team of Parent Volunteers (PVs) and
serving the school community?</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>This invitation is extended to parents who intend to register their child/ward
at Haig Girls' School under Phase 2B of the P1 Registration Exercise in
2027.</p>
<p>We are currently accepting applications for parents whose child/ward is
a Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident (SPR) and was born
between 2 January 2021 and 1 January 2022 (both dates inclusive).</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>To register your interest, please submit your application via the link:
<a href="https://form.gov.sg/635b4bb383d9150011f0aa57" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://form.gov.sg/635b4bb383d9150011f0aa57</a>
</p>
<p><strong><em>Applications open from 1 March 2026 to 30 April 2026.</em></strong>
</p>
