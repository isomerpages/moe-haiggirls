<h2><strong>ARIF BUDIMAN MALAY LANGUAGE TEACHER AWARD (AGAB) 2026</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Arif_Budiman_Malay_Language_Teacher_Award__AGAB__2026.png">
</div>
<p>The Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) 2026 recognises the
contributions of Malay Language teachers in the teaching and learning of
the Malay Language.</p>
<p>The award is open to all Malay Language teachers in the local mainstream
schools who exemplify the following criteria: &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Inspire student engagement with the Malay language, literature and culture
through creative teaching approaches and digital learning tools that foster
interactive experiences, while equipping students with 21<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;century
competencies;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate care and concern for their students' well-being, holistic
development and personal growth; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Embody the values of <em>Arif Budiman</em>, a learned person who contributes
to society through excellent and exemplary service as an educator.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Nominate your Malay Language teachers now by submitting the nomination
form at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/agab-2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/agab-2026</a> or
scanning the QR code on the e-poster. &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Nominations are open from 2 February 2026 to 13 March 2026.</strong>
</p>
<h2><strong>NOMINATION FOR MOST INSPIRING TAMIL TEACHER AWARD 2026</strong></h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Most_Inspiring_Tamil_Teacher_Award_2026.jpg">
</div>
<p>The <strong>Most Inspiring Tamil Teachers' Award</strong> recognises Tamil
Language teachers in primary and secondary schools, junior colleges and
Millennia Institute who have made significant contributions to the teaching
and learning of Tamil Language.</p>
<p>This award is open to all Tamil Language teachers in MOE schools who exemplify
the following criteria:&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Teachers who nurture students’ interest in Tamil Language by using innovative
methods, including the effective use of educational technology to make
lessons more engaging and interactive; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Dedicated and passionate teachers who demonstrate a keen interest in students’
well-being while developing 21<sup>st</sup> century competencies for them
to be future-ready. &nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Nominate your Tamil Language teachers now by submitting the nomination
form at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/mitt2026nominations" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/mitt2026nominations</a> or
scanning the QR code on the e-poster. &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Nominations are open from 2 February 2026 to 13 March 2026.</strong>
</p>
<h2><strong>NOMINATION FOR INSPIRING TEACHER OF ENGLISH AWARD 2026</strong></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Inspiring_Teacher_of_English_Award_2026.jpg">
</div>
<p>The <strong>Inspiring Teacher of English Award</strong> acknowledges teachers
who have been instrumental in igniting a love for the English Language
and are effective in helping their students communicate better. These teachers
are passionate about making English interesting and relevant to their students
and are innovative in engaging their students to help them learn English
better.</p>
<p>The Award salutes these teachers for their continual learning and constant
efforts to upgrade themselves to benefit their students and schools.</p>
<p>Nominate your English Language teachers now by submitting the nomination
form at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/iteahgs2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/iteahgs2026</a>.
&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Nominations are open from 2 February 2026 to 23 March 2026, 6 p.m.</strong>
</p>
<h2><strong>NOMINATION FOR DISTINGUISHED CHINESE LANGUAGE TEACHERS AWARDS 2026</strong></h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Distinguished_Chinese_Language_Teachers_Awards_2026.png">
</div>
<p>Organised by Lianhe Zaobao and ZBSchools, the <strong>Distinguished Chinese Teachers Awards</strong> aim
to give due recognition to deserving Chinese Language teachers who have
demonstrated exemplary efforts towards the teaching of Chinese Language
and culture over the past year. The awards also recognise the efforts of
teachers who have successfully fostered in their students an appreciation
for Chinese Language and culture.</p>
<p>All Chinese Language teachers with at least five years of teaching experience
in MOE primary, secondary, pre-university, junior colleges and IP schools
are eligible (except for past winners).</p>
<p>Nominate your Chinese Language teachers now by submitting the nomination
form at <a href="https://zb.sg/dclta2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://zb.sg/dclta2026</a> or
scanning the QR code on the e-poster. &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Nominations are open from 4 February 2026 to 30 April 2026.</strong>
</p>
