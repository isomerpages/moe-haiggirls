<h2>Parent Volunteer Scheme (P1 Admission)</h2>
<p>Join our vibrant community of Parent Volunteers (PVs) and play an active
role in supporting the school’s programmes and activities.</p>
<p>We warmly invite parents who are planning to register their child/ward
at Haig Girls' School under Phase 2B of the Primary 1 Registration Exercise
to apply. Becoming a Parent Volunteer is a meaningful way to contribute
your time, skills and support to the school while building connections
within our community.</p>
<p><strong>Application Period:</strong> 1 March to 30 April (annually). A
registration link will be published on this page during this period.</p>
<p>For further queries, you may contact the school at <a href="mailto:haiggirlssch@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>haiggirlssch@moe.edu.sg</u></a>.</p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
