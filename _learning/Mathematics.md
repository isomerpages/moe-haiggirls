<h2><strong><u>Mathematics Department</u></strong></h2>
<h3><strong>Vision</strong></h3>
<p>A community of confident and motivated students who are effective problem
solvers and team players.</p>
<h3><strong>Mission</strong></h3>
<p>To equip students with essential mathematical knowledge and skills for
everyday life and continuous learning.</p>
<h3><strong>MOE Mathematics Curriculum Framework</strong></h3>
<p>Our Mathematics curriculum is guided by the MOE framework, emphasising <strong>problem-solving as the central focus</strong>.
Students develop conceptual understanding, skills, and positive attitudes
through a structured and engaging approach.</p>

<h3><strong>Our Key Programmes:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Learning Support for Math (LSM)</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Provides early intervention for P1-P4 students to build a strong numeracy
foundation and develop confidence in Mathematics.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Talent Development Programmes</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Olympiad Training: Equips high-ability students with advanced problem-solving
techniques for Olympiad competitions.&nbsp;</p>
<p>E2K Math Programme: An enrichment programme for upper primary pupils to
foster independent learning, reasoning, and collaboration.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Math Games @ Recess</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Weekly hands-on activities led by Math Ambassadors and PSG members to
explore Mathematics in a fun and interactive way.</p>
<p><strong>Authentic Learning Experiences</strong>
<br>At our school, Mathematics comes alive through everyday experiences that
children recognise and enjoy. Students build their understanding of numbers,
measurement and problem-solving by baking, shopping and exploring real-world
situations. Whether they are measuring ingredients for a recipe, working
out costs at a set-up stall, or sharing and dividing fairly, students learn
that Mathematics is a practical skill they use every day. These authentic
experiences help students develop confidence, curiosity, and a genuine
connection to their learning.
