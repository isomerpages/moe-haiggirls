<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>To nurture Future-Ready and Responsible Digital Learners.</p>
<p>To develop designers and facilitators of quality learning experiences
through the use of ICT.</p>

<p>Department’s ICT Strategy</p>
<p>21st Century Ready Digital Leaders in an ICT-Enriched Learning Environment</p>
<p>Haig Girls’ School adopts a whole-school approach to the development of
21st century ready digital leaders in an ICT-enriched environment emphasizing
on 4 key domains:&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Curriculum, Pedagogy &amp; Assessment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupil Development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teachers’ Professional Development&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Resource Building</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Domain 1 - Curriculum, Pedagogy &amp; Assessment</p>
<p>To institutionalize the use of ICT for innovation:</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/9hkJxI1Sj9P9r56DQHlBGkHego6YO0YBEUD6_AnI-4D7FpIF2SUms-W0xR39kH-oqCu26vlpWfQC9r2TAVJd69HqrzzBDJwTfIzxKgKufqeyzowSp7dGMASzESEOhj5-yQs-DATGidLt">
</div>
<p>Domain 2 - Pupil Development</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>To develop 21st century competencies and skills in pupils through the
effective use of ICT</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop pupil leaders who can positively influence peers on safe and
responsible use of ICT</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Domain 3 - Teachers’ Professional Development</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To encourage and create opportunities for teachers to design and deliver
ICT-enriched learning experiences</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Domain 4 - Resource Building</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To support learning anytime, anywhere</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p>ICT in Action (for Teachers)</p>
<p>Different Professional Development programmes are developed according
to the 3-tiered approach:</p>

work in Professional Learning Communities, conduct peer and group observations
