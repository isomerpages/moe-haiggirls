<h2><strong><u>Vision&nbsp;</u></strong></h2>
<p>To nurture Future-Ready and Responsible Digital Learners.</p>
<p>To develop designers and facilitators of quality learning experiences
through the use of ICT.</p>
<h2><strong><u>Department’s ICT Strategy</u></strong></h2>
<h3><strong>21st Century- Ready Digital Leaders in an ICT-Enriched Learning Environment&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<p>A whole-school approach to developing 21st century-ready digital leaders
within an ICT-enriched environment is adopted, emphasising four key domains.</p>
<h2><strong><u>Domain 1 - Curriculum, Pedagogy &amp; Assessment</u></strong></h2>
<p>Meaningful integration of ICT to enhance teaching and learning across
all subject areas, empowering students with essential skills for the future.</p>

<h2><strong><u>Domain 3 - Teachers’ Professional Development</u></strong></h2>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Teachers at Haig Girls' School take part
in school-wide training, workshops, and conferences. They collaborate in
Teacher Learning Communities, observe one another’s lessons, and receive
individual coaching. These efforts support the design of meaningful, ICT-enriched
learning experiences for students.</p>
